SailFree Programme

In partnership with BP Oman and Oman Paralympic Committee, SailFree is the first parasailing programme in the GCC and MENA regions designed to introduce people with physical impairment to sailing! The ultimate aim is to form a national team to represent Oman at international sailing regattas, including the 2022 Para Sailing World Championships. Programme aims to ntroduce the candidates to the sport of para sailing ,build the skillset of the participants in order to achieve a high level of expertise in para sailing, ,develop the participants physical and innovation skills, train participants in order to overcome challenges and difficulties in the best way and create an integrated and supportive environment for athletes with disabilities.
